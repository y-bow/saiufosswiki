Welcome to the **SAIU FOSS Wiki** — the community-maintained living record of the FOSS Club at SAI University.

This wiki is where we document our events, share resources, and write about the things we're building. It's built with [Docusaurus](https://docusaurus.io/) and hosted on GitHub Pages, which means **anyone can contribute**.

{/* truncate */}

## What You'll Find Here

- **Event writeups** — recaps and photos from workshops, mapping parties, and hack nights
- **Resources** — curated FOSS tools, guides, and policy links
- **Projects** — open source projects maintained by club members
- **Blog posts** — updates, tutorials, and perspectives from club members

## How to Contribute

Fork the [wiki repo](https://github.com/saiufoss/wiki), add your content, and open a pull request. Check the [Contributing Guide](https://github.com/saiufoss/wiki/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) for details.

We're excited to build this with you. Welcome aboard!
