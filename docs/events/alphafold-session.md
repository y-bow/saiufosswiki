# Decoding Life: Understanding AlphaFold & Protein Folding

**Date:** December 13, 2025
**Format:** In-person Workshop
**Organized by:** FOSS Club, SAI University

## Overview

A student-friendly session exploring the intersection of AI, Computer Science, and Biology through the lens of AlphaFold and the protein folding problem.

## What We Covered

- Fundamentals of proteins and why the folding problem is hard
- Historical approaches to protein structure prediction
- AlphaFold's deep learning architecture and how it changed the field
- How open research and open tools have accelerated biological breakthroughs
- Real-world impact: drug discovery and scientific research

## Key Takeaway

Open-source principles enable cross-disciplinary innovation and global scientific progress. AlphaFold is one of the clearest examples of what open research can achieve.
