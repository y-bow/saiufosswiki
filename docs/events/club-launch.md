# FOSS Club Launch

**Date:** September 10, 2025
**Format:** In-person Workshop
**Organized by:** FOSS Club, Sai University

## Overview

The FOSS Club officially launched with an energetic turnout of students curious about open-source technology and community-driven development.

## What We Covered

- Core idea of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS)
- How global communities collaborate to build impactful software
- Introduction to interesting open-source projects
- Overview of the club's vision and planned activities
- Networking and discussion among students from different backgrounds

## Key Takeaway

The launch set a positive and welcoming tone, laying the foundation for a collaborative space focused on learning, sharing, and building together.
