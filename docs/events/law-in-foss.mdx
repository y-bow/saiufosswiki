# Law in FOSS: Who Owns Open Code?

**Date:** November 23, 2025  
**Format:** Online Session  
**Venue:** SAI University, Chennai

## Overview

The FOSS Club hosted a thought-provoking online session exploring 
how legal frameworks intersect with collaborative software 
development.

The discussion unpacked key ideas around copyright in software, 
clarified what "ownership" means in open source projects, and 
examined why patenting code is often impractical. Real-world cases 
such as LinkedIn vs HiQ were used to connect global legal debates 
with the Indian context.

Students gained clarity on the legal implications of sharing code 
on platforms like GitHub, and were encouraged to think critically 
about how law can both protect and shape open collaboration.
