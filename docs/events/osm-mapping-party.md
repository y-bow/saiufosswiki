# OpenStreetMap Mapping Party

<span class="badge badge--primary">Upcoming</span>

## What is OpenStreetMap?

[OpenStreetMap (OSM)](https://www.openstreetmap.org) is a free, collaborative, open-source map of the world. Think of it as the Wikipedia of maps — anyone can contribute, edit, and improve the data.

## What We'll Do

At this mapping party, we will:

1. Learn the basics of editing OpenStreetMap
2. Survey and map local points of interest around the Sai University campus
3. Add building footprints, roads, and landmarks
4. Contribute to India's geospatial commons

## Who Can Join?

Everyone! No prior experience with mapping or GIS is needed. We will walk you through the tools during the session.

## How to Prepare

- Create an account at [openstreetmap.org/user/signup](https://www.openstreetmap.org/user/signup)
- Install the **StreetComplete** app ([Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.westnordost.streetcomplete) / [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/streetcomplete/id1491507609))
- Bring a laptop or smartphone with internet access
- Optionally install the [ID Editor](https://www.openstreetmap.org/edit) browser extension
