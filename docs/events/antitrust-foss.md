# Antitrust Implications of FOSS: A Review

**Date:** November 20, 2025
**Format:** In-person Workshop
**Organized by:** FOSS Club, SAI University

## Overview

A session exploring how competition law interacts with FOSS ecosystems in India, with a focus on policy literacy for developers.

## What We Covered

- Fundamentals of the Competition Act (India)
- The Google Android antitrust case — lessons for FOSS
- Questions around fairness, dominance, and openness in tech markets
- How the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) impacts FOSS projects and developer responsibilities

## Key Takeaway

Policy literacy is increasingly essential for developers and technologists. Understanding how law influences innovation and corporate involvement in open source is part of being an informed contributor.
